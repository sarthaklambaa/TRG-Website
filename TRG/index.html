    About us

    Our Establishment

    We are three friends, who love dogs and are actually obsessed with them. Each of us has a pet mainly a dog and we are working, so we hardly got any time for the grooming session for our pets. So, here we decided to start a van which grooms on wheels.We mainly commenced this van for people who hardly get any time to get their pets groomed.This van is custom designed especially for the grooming of dogs/cats. We provide door-to-door services and hence you do not have to be tensed about the grooming and cleaning of your pets. All the groomers are professional and we use the best quality products only. We provide you with the best and most reasonable rates in the town. We are soon going to expand our areas.

    Trained Professionals

    Amazing Support
